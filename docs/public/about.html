My Bio

Hello! My name is Nicolás Martínez Djekic, I am 18 years old and a true blend of cultures. Born and raised in Spain, I carry the vibrant heritage of being half Colombian and half Serbian, with British nationality. This unique mix has shaped who I am and the way I see the world. If I had to sum myself up in three words, they'd be funny, easy-going, and creative-qualities that drive my approach to both life and work.


I'm passionate about basketball and proud to be a part of my university's basketball team. The court is where I feel most alive, and I enjoy the game and challenges it brings. When I'm not shooting hoops, I immerse myself in music. Whether it's producing or just listening, music is my escape and a constant source of inspiration. It fuels my creativity, and I'm always exploring new sounds and ways to express myself.


I'm always excited to connect with new people and experience what the world has to offer, constantly learning and growing along the way.
